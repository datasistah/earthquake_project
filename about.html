Motivated by the recent magnitude 5.1 earthquake which struck North Carolina on August 9th, 2020, we decided to take a look back in history and explore the frequency, magnitudes, and locations of these natural events.


After reviewing several datasets, we came across a dataset collecting data on significant earthquakes around the world since 1600 from the U.S. Department of Commerce, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration available through Kaggle.
