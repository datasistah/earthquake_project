About Our Scatter Plot



Our scatter plot titled Earthquake Count by Year shows the number of earthquakes recorded per each year within our dataset.

The more we approach the 21st century, the more earthquake occurrences which are also observed.

This is most likely due to the vast advancements in technology which have allowed for more precise monitoring and recording of earthquake events.
